Ingredients:
 - 1 1/2 cups SRF
 - 1 cup oatmeal
 - 1 Tbsp caster sugar
 - 1 tsp baking powder
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 1/4 cup milk
 - 60g softened butter
 - 1 tsp vanilla essence
 - 2 small mashed bananas
 - Maple syrup (to serve)
 - Yogurt (to serve)

Directions:
 1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. Make a well in the centre.
 
 2. Whisk eggs, milk, butter and vanilla essence in a bowl. 
     Add mixture and bananas to dry ingredents. 
     Gently mix together with a soft spatula.

 3. Add approximately 1/4 cup helpings of the mixture into a low-medium heat frying pan. 
     Cook for 2-3mins each side.
 
 4. Serve with maple syrup and yogurt.
